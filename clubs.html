First Port City Youth Leadership

The First Port City Youth Leadership club is a six month program that trains participants in soft skills, leadership training, and hands on experience with local professionals.

I joined this club in the 11th grade, through this club I learned leadership skills, communication skills, how to make a resume, and how to greet people. We also ran a local shoe drive.

Bearcat Voice

The Bearcat Voice is a student organization that helps to maintain school spirit and helps fellow students.

I joined the Bearcat Voice in the 9th grade, while in this club we did peer reviews for student essays, ran multiple bake sales, and helped make a booklet composed of student submitted poems.

TSA

TSA is a national organization of students engaged in STEM

I joined TSA after a teacher reccomended it to me, through this club I have participated in multiple blood drives.

Quiz Bowl

Quiz Bowl is a game in which two teams compete head-to-head to asnwer questions from all areas of knowledge: history, literature, science, fine arts, current events, pop culture, sports, and more.

I participated in multiple tournaments while in Quiz Bowl and participated in out of school functions relating to Quiz Bowl.
