About Me

Jared Davis is an incoming freshman at the University of Georgia, who received his High School Diploma from Bainbridge High School in May of 2022. Jared can be described as an incredibly fierce student athlete, having received the High School Heisman award for his many athletic accomplishments, and as a devout scholar, given his acceptance into the National Honor Society. Jared is a well rounded student, with a diverse selection of Extracurricular activities and club involvement.

However, Jared isn't just a well accomplished student, he is also an extremely loyal friend who is an absolute joy to get to know and hang out with. Jared has his intellectual side, but once you get to know him, you can find out how much fun he can be as well.

At UGA, Jared intends to pursue his degree in engineering.
